Redefining Visual Presentation

DualVisor AI Gallery Enhanced is engineered from the ground up to solve the specific needs of digital artists, prompt engineers, and retouchers. We noticed that traditional portfolios fail to communicate the iterative, transformative nature of AI art generation.

With a focus on performance, mental models, and modern UX guidelines, DualVisor introduces a seamless visual comparison engine. By prioritizing interaction through tactile sliders and dynamic aspect-ratio support, it respects your artwork's original composition while delivering a premium viewing experience.

Core Features

  • Interactive Comparison Slider: Smooth, intuitive touch-enabled slider specifically designed to highlight original and AI-modified image differences.
  • Adaptive Layouts: Complete elimination of black bars. The container natively adapts to portrait and landscape orientations without breaking the user experience.
  • Smart Folder Organization: Keep large generative datasets organized in collapsible, beautifully rendered category shelves.
  • Modern & Minimal UI: Designed with accessibility in mind, featuring clear focus states, high-contrast typography, and intuitive touch targets.
